Hey, dear Kashish Jain! There are countless reasons why I've fallen in love with you. Here are some cherished moments that I want to put into words, which I remember fondly

Journey :

(1) I hold vivid memories of our college conducting classes online during our first semester. Little did I know when I sent you that very first message, that you would become such an indispensable part of my life. Our initial conversation revolved around introductions, asking about each other, our whereabouts, and even suggesting that you could join us for a meet-up. Back then, my impression of you was casual, not paying meticulous attention to every detail. But now, as I pen down these words, our relationship has flourished over the past six months, and our journey together feels like a captivating movie. I recollect the times when my college friends and I made plans to visit the campus, and I would occasionally ask if we should meet there, considering the online nature of our classes. However, you would decline, embracing your introverted nature 😂.

Kashish and I have been enrolled in the BCA department at St. Xavier's College since 2021. Despite our differing backgrounds, with Kashish belonging to the upper middle class and myself hailing from the middle-middle class, we have embarked on a remarkable journey together. This narrative encapsulates our experiences from the first year to the third year starting days. It was during the first week of January 2023 when we mustered the courage to confess our feelings for each other.
